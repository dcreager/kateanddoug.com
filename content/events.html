Events

Pre-wedding festivities

We'll try to plan some interesting activities for the days leading up to the wedding. Also, the wedding takes place the weekend after the Fourth of July; if you haven't seen the fireworks over the Charles River, you should!

That whole wedding thing

We will be having an outdoor ceremony on the East Lawn of the Commandant’s House in Charlestown, Massachusetts. After the ceremony, we will be hosting a cocktail hour inside the Commandant’s House, where you will be able to enjoy some of the detailed architecture of this great historical site. We will be having our tented reception on the West Lawn of the Commandant’s House, where you will be able to enjoy views of the USS Constitution, the Boston Harbor and the Boston skyline.
