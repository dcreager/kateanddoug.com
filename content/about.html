About us

Doug

As Doug will tell you (in far too much detail), he was born in Florida, then moved to Minnesota for 11 years, then to North Carolina, and arrived in Boston for college. Doug crossed the pond for 3 years while studying for his PhD in England, and then finally came back to Boston, the city he loves.

Doug currently works out of his home office, using one of his six computers. In his spare time, Doug likes to cheer on the Red Sox (in person and on TV), cook, read, and most of all, hang out with Kate.

Kate

Kate was born and raised in Clinton, Massachusetts, which is in Central (not Western!) Mass. After a short stint in upstate New York, Kate moved to Boston for law school and has stayed there ever since.

Kate currently works in Wellesley, doing some form of lawyering. In her free time, Kate likes to cook, bake apple pies, hang out with her friends and family, and most of all, hang out with Doug.
